Wyatt C. Raymond

Humble Beginnings

Wyatt was born December 26th 1996 in Yuma Arizona, however it would not be long before his family would move to Maryland in the year of 2000. Wyatt grew up in a small town named Rising Sun that was just south of the Maryland-Pennsylvania border and went to Rising Sun schools for elementry, middle and high school, in which he graduated in 2014. Growing up, Wyatt was very invovled with the Boy Scouts and earned the rank of Eagle at 16 years old.

Virginia Military Institute

Upon graduation of High School, Wyatt was accepted into the Virginia Military Institute in Lexington VA and was awarded an Army ROTC Scholorship. He arrived at the Institute on August of 2014 and began his four grueling years of military education. Despite the numerous hardships that Wyatt and his classmates ahd to endure, He will forever cherish the friends and memories that were made during his cadetship. On May 16th 2018, Wyatt graduated with his B.S. in Electrical Engineering and commissioned as a Second Lieutenant into the United States Army as an Air Defense Artillery Officer.

Finding Love

During his time at VMI, Wyatt met then Hope Grigg, who was attending Liberty University in Lynchburg VA. After inviting her to be his date to his Third Class Midwinter formal, the two began to grow closer and develop a serious relationship. The fall of thier senior year of college, Wyatt proposed to Hope and they got married following graduation in her home town of Bedford VA.

United States Army

Wyatt entered Active Duty in July of 2018 and began his Army career with Basic Offficer Leadership Course located at Fort Sill Oklahoma. After around four months of schooling, He reported to his first duty station, Fort Hood TX, in January of 2019. While Assigned to Fort Hood, Wyatt served as a Platoon Leader in Patriot Battery. Wyatt learned alot about leadership and himself while in this postion and is thankful for the oppurtunites he was given to lead Soldiers. His current assignment is located at Fort Sill OK, where he currently serves as an Executive Officer in a Basic Training Battery.
